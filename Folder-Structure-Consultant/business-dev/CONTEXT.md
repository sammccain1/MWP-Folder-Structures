# business-dev/ — Business Development

You are in the **business development directory**. This tracks the consulting pipeline, outreach, and case study assets used to win new engagements.

## Subdirectory Guide

| Directory | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `pipeline/` | Active and prospective engagement tracking. Use a running `pipeline.md` or a simple CSV to track status, contact, and next steps. |
| `outreach/` | Outreach templates and logs. Cold emails, LinkedIn messages, follow-up sequences. |
| `case-studies/` | Anonymized write-ups of past projects for use in proposals. Highlight outcomes and methodologies. |

## Rules for This Directory

- Pipeline data stays internal — never commit sensitive contact data or private BD notes
- Case studies must be anonymized: no client names, specific data, or identifiable outcomes without permission
- Outreach templates should be personalization-ready — leave `[NAME]` / `[COMPANY]` / `[ROLE]` hooks
- Track pipeline status with clear stages: `Prospecting → Contacted → In Discussion → Proposal Sent → Won/Lost`
- BD activity here feeds into `templates/Proposals/` — keep them in sync when methodology evolves
